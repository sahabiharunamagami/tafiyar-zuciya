Chapter 1 – Farkon Tafiya

Bismillahir Rahmanir Rahim.

Wannan shi ne farkon littafin Mun Tashi Tare... Ƙaddara Ta Raba Mu. A nan ne za mu fara saka cikakken Chapter 1 da muka rubuta a baya, cikin tsari mai kyau domin mai karatu ya ji kamar yana amfani da ƙwararriyar manhajar karatu.

A sigar farko, wannan shafin zai zama tushen karatun littafin. Daga baya za mu saka cikakken rubutun Chapter 1, sannan mu haɗa shi da Chapter 2 har zuwa Chapter 15.
